# TeaTime
#### Authors: Cindy, Varshini, Keira
#### Revision: May 23, 2026

# Introduction:
You have been trapped by a mysterious person, forcing you to run a boba shop! If you wish for
your freedom back, you must earn 500 coins by selling drinks to various customers. Keep in mind
you’d want to make drinks of the highest quality possible—if you make the wrong drink, the customer
will get angry and pay less money!
To play, you must first take the order from the customer. Then, choose the right amount and
flavor of toppings specified. Then, click the right ice & sugar level. Next, fill the drink with the right
flavor and amount of tea. Lastly, poke the straw in the center! Be careful to click the correct buttons,
as if you wish to restart, you must discard the drink entirely (which will lower your score). After serving
the drink to the customer, they will score it based on how tasty it is, and will give you the number of
stars your drink deserves. It will also be reflected in how much they pay.
Our program aims to entertain the user, as well as enhancing cognitive skills, such as the
following: processing speed, memory, and attention span. It also provides a way to destress, as you
make simple boba drinks for cute customers while having the ability to learn stories about each
character. (Oh my, the lore!) We wrote this program as we felt it’s a relaxing and enjoyable game we
would like to play ourselves.
Furthermore, our game specifically targets teenagers, though it is open to all! It’s aimed
towards those who enjoy food games, like Papa’s Pizzeria, as well as working under light pressure to
complete orders. This is a game for people who like completing tasks, as well as reading side stories.
It has a level of difficulty, to pose a challenge for the user, but it is not competitive, and rather
slow-paced.
The primary features of our program focus mostly on the boba-making process, as well as our
customers. The screen is split into two: an ordering station, and kitchen (which consists of several
buttons for each step of the boba-making process). While making the drink, customers may start
talking about their life, interests, or just fun facts! See if you can follow along, but don’t let it mess up
your drink-making process! We hope that you enjoy playing our game as much as we enjoyed making
it!

# Instructions:
This game is a cute cozy bubble tea shop…
To play, you take an order from the customer, and do your best to create it based on their requests.
You click on buttons to perform actions (choosing ice level, sugar level, tea flavor, and toppings). The
arrow keys serve no purpose, nor does any key, in the boba making process. The mouse is the key.
When creating boba, you must follow the correct steps. First, you must add the topping(s): click the
scooper to pick it up, click the topping you want to scoop, and click over the cup to drop the toppings
in. Then, repeat for as many times as required to reach the desired amount. Next, select the ice level
by clicking the correct percentage. After each action, the previous section will be locked and you
won’t be able to go back to change it or add more. After choosing the ice level, choose how much
sugar. Then, choose the correct tea flavor and hold the button (the flavor) on the drink dispenser so
that the liquid reaches the desired level. Let go to stop the flow. After completing the drink, click the
done button and poke the straw in the center of the drink to finish. If an error was made, to discard
the drink, click the trash button and the cup will be emptied out. Click on the customer to serve the
drink, and find out how well you did.
The amount of coins you receive will reflect your work, as it is scored using each section’s accuracy,
and the amount of tries it took to complete. To store your coins, click on the cash register.
During the boba making process, the customer might “spill tea” (talk), so pay attention!
To win the game, you must earn 500 coins. Good luck!
